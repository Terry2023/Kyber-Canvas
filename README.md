# Kyber Canvas

*An Open-Source Framework for Systemic Observability*

**Kyber Canvas** is a diagnostic engine that moves business strategy from static, scalar planning to dynamic, tensor-based observability. It treats organizational business models as coupled networks, identifying "structural snaps"—the moment when internal coherence degrades—long before they manifest as market or operational failures.

## The Core Problem: The Scalar Blindspot
Standard business analysis relies on scalar metrics (Revenue, Churn, P&L). These metrics are retrospective and isolated. They ignore the **tensor-coupling** between operational nodes. In complex environments, failure is not a sudden event—it is an emergent property of structural misalignment that builds over time.

## The Methodology: Rank-3 Tensor Coupling
Kyber Canvas employs a rank-3 tensor field to measure the "stretch" between business drivers. We define coherence as a quantifiable metric: when the interdependent nodes of a business lose their structural independent behavior, the system is at risk of cascading failure.

## Proof of Concept
Our diagnostic engine has been back-tested against historical and systemic failure models:

* **Black Monday (1987) Analysis**: Our tensor model identified the structural snap in market coherence 17 weeks prior to the price collapse.
* **COBOL Legacy Crisis**: A tensor-stress terrain simulation used to map technical debt and migration runway across legacy organizational workflows.

## Key Features
* **Agentic Governance ("Bezos Auditor")**: An integrated AI auditor that provides explainable, citeable diagnostics for structural risk.
* **Environmental Stress-Testing**: Simulate your business model against varying external regimes (macroeconomic shocks, competitive pressure, resource depletion).
* **High-Performance Engine**: A hybrid C++/Python stack designed for real-time observability in high-stakes environments.
